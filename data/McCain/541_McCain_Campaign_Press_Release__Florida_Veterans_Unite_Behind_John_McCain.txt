TALLAHASSEE, FL -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced the leadership of Florida Veterans for John McCain, a statewide grassroots organization that will work to bolster John McCain's support within Florida's veterans' community."John McCain has the leadership, judgment and experience to keep Americans safe at home and abroad in these difficult times," said Chair of Florida Veterans for McCain USAF Colonel Bud Day (Ret.), a longtime friend and fellow Vietnam POW with McCain.
