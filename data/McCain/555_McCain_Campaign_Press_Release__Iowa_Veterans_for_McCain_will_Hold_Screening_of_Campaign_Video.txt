DES MOINES, IA -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Iowa Veterans for John McCain will host a screening ofthe campaign video entitled, "The Obama Iraq Documentary: Whatever the Politics Demand" on Tuesday, July 22nd. Iowa Veterans for McCain Chairman Major General, (Ret.) Evan (Curly) Hultman and Vice Chairman Colonel Charles (Chuck) Larson Sr. will speak.
