ALBUQUERQUE, NM -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that it will be launching the New Mexico Veterans for McCain Coalition in Las Cruces on Tuesday, July 22nd at 12:00 p.m. MDT. This announcement occurs in conjunction with the announcement of the Statewide Veterans for McCain Steering Committee and Veterans for McCain rallies across Nevada and the country.
