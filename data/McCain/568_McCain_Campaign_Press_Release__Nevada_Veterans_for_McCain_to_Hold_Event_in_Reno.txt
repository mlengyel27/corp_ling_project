LAS VEGAS, NV -- U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Robert Waggoner (Major, USAF, Ret. and POW in Vietnam with Senator McCain); Michael A. Carrigan (USN, Ret., City of Sparks City Councilman); and Tim R. Shepard (former Army Sergeant and Paratrooper) will participate in the announcement of Nevada Veterans for McCain on Tuesday, July 22nd at 11:00 a.m. PDT.
