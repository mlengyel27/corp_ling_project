TALLAHASSEE, FL --U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced its North Carolina Small Business Leaders for McCain coalition chairs and steering committee. Small business owners throughout North Carolina endorsed John McCain's economic plan today, joining business leaders across the country who have praised John McCain's Jobs for America economic plan to grow the economy as president."I support John McCain's jobs-first agenda because he will ensure that small businesses aren't overburdened with taxes," said State Representative Harold Brubaker. "John McCain's tax policy will allow America's small businesses to prosper and grow. "Sue Weems, former president of the Greater Raleigh chapter of National Association of Women Business Owners added, "John McCain will help to ensure that the millions of women who own small businesses across the country have the opportunity to succeed.
