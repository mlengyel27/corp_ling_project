MANCHESTER, NH --U.S. Senator John McCain's presidential campaign today announced that Former Pennsylvania Governor and Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security Tom Ridge will participate in the announcement of New Hampshire Veterans for John McCain on Thursday, July 17th.
